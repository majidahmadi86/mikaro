OptiClean — a 19th-century brand, shipped in days.

From a single reference image to a complete bilingual store with a working checkout.

Client

OptiClean by Dr. Zac — eyewear care

Scope

10 pages · cart · Stripe checkout · SEO

Languages

French · English — EUR / CHF

Status

Live at opticlean.mikaro.studio ↗

The brief

Dr. Zac had a brand with a soul — a vintage French apothecary character, a marigold label, a gentleman on the bottle — and one reference image. The task: prove the brand could live online, pixel-faithful, with commerce that actually works.

What shipped

  • Ten pages, fully bilingual FR/EN with automatic language detection
  • EUR / CHF price switching for the French and Swiss markets
  • A live cart with free-shipping logic and a working Stripe checkout — test it with card 4242 4242 4242 4242
  • Story, product, FAQ, contact and complete legal pages, plus schema and social cards

The client's words

“It looks absolutely beautiful… your demo has given me every confidence that you're the right person for this project.” — Dr. Zac, Founder
