Miomika — a cat that teaches you to talk.

A voice-first AI companion where Miomi the cat teaches Thai and English through real conversation.

Role

Product, design, engineering — everything

Stack

LLM teaching brain · TTS pipeline · Stripe

Languages

Thai · English

Status

Live at miomika.com ↗

The idea

Language apps drill flashcards; people learn by talking. Miomika puts a character in the middle — Miomi, a kawaii cat — and teaches Thai and English through actual voice conversation. You speak, Miomi listens, corrects gently, and answers back with real pronunciation.

What's under the fur

  • A teaching brain built on an LLM, tuned for gentle correction and bilingual switching
  • A full speech pipeline — recognition in, natural TTS out — engineered for cost guardrails
  • Payments with a referral-credit engine, so learners bring learners
  • An admin console: pipeline health, cost monitoring, content control

Why it matters for your project

Miomika is the proof that this studio ships the hard parts: real AI in production, real payments, real operations — not a demo that dies after the pitch.
