Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does a logo design cost?
Logo packages are customised to your needs — from a single mark to a full CI/CIG brand identity system with guidelines and all file formats. Contact me for a free quote based on your scope.
How long does a website take to build?
A single-page professional website typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full multi-page business site takes 3–4 weeks. I keep you updated throughout with clear milestones.
Can you do the logo and website together?
Absolutely — and it's the best approach. When designed together, everything stays perfectly consistent. Combined brand + web packages are also better value than separate projects.
Do you work with international clients remotely?
Yes — I work with clients across Thailand and internationally via Zoom, WhatsApp, or email. Design and marketing projects are delivered fully remotely with no issues.
What industries do you work with?
F&B, retail, education, tech startups, professional services, hospitality, healthcare, and personal brands. Good design and marketing adapts to any industry — I tailor the approach to your context.
What's the best way to get started?
Message me on WhatsApp or fill in the contact form below. I offer a free 30-minute discovery call to discuss your project — no obligation, just an honest conversation about what's possible.
